# Inconclusive Target-QA Checklist

Source: `inconclusive-target-qa.json`.

## Required Facts

- Lead with inconclusive from `copy_assessment.state = "inconclusive"`.
- Explain that target quality blocked hard support from
  `copy_assessment.blockers = ["target_quality_not_passed"]`.
- Ask for improved target alignment from `copy_assessment.reshoot = ["improve_target_alignment"]`.
- State that keystone or target geometry can fake corner asymmetry.

## Forbidden Claims

- Do not call the lens centred.
- Do not call the lens decentred.
- Do not infer that alignment failure means the copy is bad.
- Do not give keep/return advice.
