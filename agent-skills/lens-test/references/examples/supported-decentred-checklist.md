# Supported Decentred Checklist

Source: `supported-decentred.json`.

## Required Facts

- Lead with decentred support from `copy_assessment.state = "supports_decentred"`.
- Say the support comes from target-QA-gated left/right acutance asymmetry, not from agent-side
  measurement.
- Mention that field-curvature counterevidence passed, so the CLI did not find field curvature to be
  the stronger explanation.
- Mention that pair deltas exceed the decentred-support threshold.

## Forbidden Claims

- Do not give keep/return advice.
- Do not say this is a legal or purchase recommendation.
- Do not say the agent recalculated the asymmetry.
- Do not ask for a reshoot when `copy_assessment.reshoot` is empty.
