# Inconclusive Aperture-Ladder Checklist

Source: `inconclusive-aperture-ladder.json`.

## Required Facts

- Lead with inconclusive from `copy_assessment.state = "inconclusive"`.
- Explain that the aperture series is insufficient from
  `copy_assessment.blockers = ["insufficient_aperture_series"]`.
- Ask for the missing aperture ladder from `copy_assessment.reshoot = ["add_aperture_ladder"]`.
- Name the intended ladder: f/4, f/5.6, f/8, and f/11 when applicable.

## Forbidden Claims

- Do not say the copy is centred just because the available frame does not prove decentring.
- Do not say the copy is decentred from one aperture group.
- Do not imply missing data is a positive result.
- Do not give keep/return advice.
